My Kheer Recipe

This is my special Kheer recipe that I didn't just google

What you'll get from this recipe

  • Delicious kheer
  • Motivation to tackle anything in life
  • A sugar rush
  • Impress your mother-in-law

What you'll need

  • 1/4 cup Basmati rice (any starchy rice is fine)
  • 1 liter Full fat milk
  • 4-6 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 mins
  2. Pour 1/4 cup water or grease into a heavy bottom pot
  3. When it turns hot add drained rice
  4. Stir frequently to avoid burning. Keep flame on low/medium
  5. Cook until rice is soft and slightly mushy
  6. Add sugar
  7. Lower flame and cook until it thickens
  8. Turn off stove when it becomes thick
  9. Enjoy!
